Welcome to our website!

Here at Coolio Games™ we are dedicated to producing interactive puzzle games for children. Originally we were founded under the name of Caveson Toys in 1970 by our founder John Caveson as a toy company branching from Focal Science Inc. However, in 1990 John unfortunately passed away and his daughter Freya inherited the company. In 1991 Freya changed the company's name to Coolio Games™, taking her father's company into the digital world. Since then we have produced many games for children globally, winning multiple awards. Now Coolio Games™ has been run by Freddie since 2021 and he continues to push this company forward in his grandfather's vision.

Here is a list of awards we have won:

  • 2021 Best Educational Game at D4
  • 2005 British Video Game Awards - Best Children's Game
  • 2002 British Universities Technology Tournament (Runners Up)
  • 1993 Ray William Johnson Memorial Prize
  • 1992 Nobbel Computer Science Prize
  • 1991 British Universities Technology Tournament (1st place)
  • 1991 Black Mesa Innovation in Education Award
