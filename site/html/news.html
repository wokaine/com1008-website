30 Year Anniversary!

13/11/2021

It has been 30 years since Freya Butterfield turned her fathers company into Coolio Games, taking it to win multiple awards. We at Coolio Games are celebrating the event with pizza bought using Magic Adventure II's budget! However it can't be party time all the time... we are still working very hard on finalizing Magic Adventure II. A statement from Freya: "We've had 30 years of excellence, let's make it 31. The budget? We will simply make money"

MAII Development Update

02/10/2021

Magic Adventure II is on its way! We have made a lot of progress since the D4 demonstration and have added many new features that users are sure to enjoy. One of the features we are most excited about is the revamped turn-based combat system from the first game, but unfortunately we can't say too much about it. We hope that everyone will be able to enjoy the game, and it is set to release on the 14th of December. At this stage we are currently ironing out bugs so that the game is perfect when it comes out. CEO Freddie Butterfield says: "This is our most ambitious project since Scrimbly Binbo 5, I think this is a game that both kids and adults will enjoy."

Back to School Sale!

Coolio Games is having a back to school sale! Coolio Games Members that have access to our store can get games for up to 50% off for school use and 25% off for personal use. Success at this year's D4

28/07/2021

This years D4 Expo was a massive success for Coolio Games, our upcoming game Magic Adventure II was demonstrated and won the "Best Educational Award" at the end of the show award ceremony. Everyone at Coolio Games has worked incredibly hard to make sure this is one of our best games yet, so when Freddie Butterfield gave the news to the company they were ecstatic and are now more motivated then ever to develop the game.
